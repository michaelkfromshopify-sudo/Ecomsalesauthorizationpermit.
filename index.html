What is an E-Commerce Sales Authorization Permit (ESAP)?

ESAP is an official certification that verifies your e-commerce business as compliant with platform requirements and builds trust with customers.

Why ESAP Matters for Your Online Store

An ESAP serves as proof that your business has been vetted and authorized to sell online. It helps prevent account suspensions during platform audits and builds credibility with customers who increasingly demand verified sellers.

Many payment processors and marketplaces are now requiring seller permits to combat fraud and ensure compliance with local regulations.

  • Verified authorization for e-commerce platforms
  • Reduced risk of account suspensions
  • Increased customer trust and conversions
  • Faster approval for payment processors

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the seller permit.

An ESAP is an official document that verifies your business as a legitimate e-commerce seller. It helps with platform compliance, builds customer trust, and may be required for certain payment processors.

Most applications are processed within 3-5 business days. Expedited processing is available for Professional and Enterprise plans.

While not currently required by Shopify, having an ESAP can help prevent account suspensions during compliance checks and may be required by certain payment processors you connect to your store.

Basic applications require your business registration (if applicable) and government ID. Professional and Enterprise plans may require additional documentation depending on your business type.
